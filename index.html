CITY

Hebron

Hebron, also known as Al-Khalil in Arabic, is a historically rich city located in the West Bank of Palestine. It holds immense cultural and religious significance,dating back over 4,000 years. Hebron is renowned for its well-preserved Old City, characterized by narrow, winding streets, ancient architecture, and bustling markets. One of its most iconic sites is the Cave of the Patriarchs, a holy place revered by both Jews and Muslims, as it is said to be the burial site of Abraham and his family. While Hebron's history is marked by complexity and conflict due to its strategic location, it remains a place where history,and contemporary life intersect.
